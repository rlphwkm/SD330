About MaristParks

MaristParks is a parking management app designed for Marist College. It provides information about parking lots, availability, and campus navigation, aiming to enhance parking convenience.

All data, including parking availability, operational hours, and security levels, is for demonstration purposes only and may not reflect real conditions.

Key features include:

  • Overview of parking lots with total and available spaces.
  • Interactive search for parking lots based on key attributes.
  • An accurate campus map for navigation (displayed as an image).
  • 404 page to guide users back to the homepage if a page is not found.
